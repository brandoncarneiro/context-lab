# Customization Guide

Context Lab works best when adapted to role and use case.

## Founder or operator

Emphasize business objectives, decision cadence, stakeholders, metrics, constraints, and recurring workflows.

## Executive or manager

Emphasize team structure, operating rhythm, communication preferences, decision rights, and management risks.

## Consultant or freelancer

Emphasize client types, project workflow, positioning, deliverable style, sales motion, and boundaries.

## Creator or writer

Emphasize audience, voice, publishing cadence, taste, topic territory, editing preferences, and idea capture workflow.

## Student or researcher

Emphasize field of study, learning goals, research methods, source standards, deadlines, and feedback preferences.

## Personal productivity user

Emphasize routines, constraints, planning style, health or travel preferences, household logistics, and recurring decisions.
